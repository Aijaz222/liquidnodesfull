    What is rebase token?

    A rebase token is a crypto asset that changes in circulating supply, either growing larger (more coins being created) or decreasing (coins get destroyed or ‘burnt’). The increase or decrease of the market cap is spread proportionally across token holders.

    What is APY?

    APY stands for “Annual Percentage Yield”. This means that you get paid interest every year on the amount in your account. The higher the APY, the better.

    APY is traditionally calculated using the amount of money deposited into your account and the length of time you have had access to the account. For example, if you deposit $1,000 into a savings account earning 2% APY, then you would earn $20 per month or $240 per year. If you leave the account open for five years, you would receive $2,400 in interest.

    In the case of Freedom, APY is based on the number of $FDM tokens you have in your wallet, instead of the value of these $FDM tokens.

    Every 30 minutes, a “rebase event” occurs, which adds more $FDM tokens to the existing tokens in your wallet (compounding interest). After a rebase event, the quantity of $FDM tokens in your wallet grows (previous $FDM balance + compound interest). The number of tokens in your wallet after a rebase event becomes your new balance, which the next rebase event is calculated on.

    For example, if you start with 1,000 $FDM tokens on day 1, you will have 500,000 $FDM tokens in your wallet after 365 days.

    Why are there taxes on buying and selling $FDM?

    Taxes incentivize long-term holding and reward you for investing in the project. Here’s how:

    Taxes prevent panic selling

    Investors have will have to carefully consider if they are willing to pay a ~20% tax to sell their tokens, or continue earning 50,000% interest by keeping them.

    Taxes encourage HODLing

    When someone sells $FDM, the tax is distributed to everyone else holding $FDM. Since rewards are based on the amount of $FDM you hold and compound every 30 minutes, it becomes very lucrative to continue holding as many tokens as you can.

    Taxes prevents abusive whale attacks, bots, and day traders from influencing the price.

    Obviously, a ~10% tax may not be much to an abusive whale, but even trying to trade twice would result in roughly a ~50% tax even if they made profits. It just isn’t wise to daytrade Freedom.
